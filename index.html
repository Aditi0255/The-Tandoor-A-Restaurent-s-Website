About Us

Know Abour Our Story

where taste meets the Tradition

Our purpose is simple and bold. We exist to redefine how Indian restaurants taste, feel, and look. Our founding values consist ofproviding clean and heart-warming food prepared with ethical practices with a hugeimportance on our team members and ourguests. We believe the happiness that comesfrom feeding souls is worth sharing. We strive to positively impact the communities we live in and constantly practice our values. It is our pleasure to serve you.Be brave and try something new.
Our services include-

  • Dine In- Experience the tradition.
  • Take Away- Enjoy it to-go .
  • Catering- Show off your taste.

Our sincere effort is to make your dining experience memorable by bringing to you centuries old Cuisine, and recipes from our mother’s kitchen. For us meal time is more than food-it's about connection and sharing love.

Why Choose Us?

We specialize in Indian breads(Naan), meats grilled in tandoor, variety of curries,and biryani. and aim to provide an excellent choice of various exotic vegetables, lentils, breads, aromatic rice, meat and much more. Our extensive menu includes a fine collection of wine and spirits in addition to well-known traditional Indian dishes.
